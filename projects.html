Safe Water

Hayatu Charity Foundation has provided a borehole to supply clean and safe water to the residents of Nakuwadde Bbira in Wakiso District, Central Region. This initiative aims to address the community's water needs and improve their quality of life.

The Foundation is committed to expanding this effort by constructing more boreholes in other regions, ensuring access to clean water for many more communities in need.
