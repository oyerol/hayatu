History

The Hayatu Charity Foundation was established to support vulnerable communities in Uganda, focusing on orphans and widows.

Since its founding in 2020, it has provided education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, transforming lives through various initiatives and community partnerships to promote sustainable development and hope for a brighter future.


Our Vision

"A World where every individual lives with dignity opportunity, and hope."

Our Mission

"To transform lives through innovative solutions, humanitarian assistance, and capacity-building programs that promote self-reliance."
